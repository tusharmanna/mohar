About Mohar

A Vision without a Strategy remains an Illusion

Mohar plans individual training for all age group to increase their discipline and analytical skills.

Mohar Chess is a chess training institute led by Ms. Mohua Maity, with 10 years of teaching experience. Mohua is a certified tournament director who has organized several local and state scholastic tournaments. She also uses Chess as a powerful therapeutic tool to unlock the possibilities for young minds of special needs children.

Mohua Maity, with extensive experience in teaching Chess since 2014, has been associated with schools like Montessori School of Cumming and Cornerstone School. Currently working with Montessori Academy at Sharon Springs, The Goddard School in Georgia, USA.

At Mohar Chess, we recognize and celebrate the uniqueness of each individual. Our commitment to individualized training ensures that every student's specific needs are not just met, but fully understood and catered to. This approach not only enhances discipline and analytical skills but also fosters patience, vision, leadership, and confidence in our students' actions and decision making.

Led by Mohua, the owner of Mohar Chess, we also have Joseph Couvillion, and two more highly skilled assistant coaches. Their expertise and dedication ensure that every student receives the best possible training.

Mohar Chess organizer monthly USCF rated tournaments for all age groups and all sections. Mohar Chess organizes workshops for senior citizens and kids with special needs.

Mohar Chess (USCF id A8555044) is proud to be affiliated with the United States Chess Federation (USCF) and Georgia Chess Association (GCA).
