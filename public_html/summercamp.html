Summer Camp 2025

Age Group - 7 to 13
Dates - June 9 - 13
Time - 9:30 am to 4:30 pm
Entry Fee - $350
Location - TBD

After a successful July summer camp last year, Mohar devised a new plan to make our young minds think out of the box, be creative with analytical skills, and be a confident leaders. They will build things, set up their shops, advertise their business, and learn the math of gains and losses.

By the end of the week, your child will have gained valuable skills in decision-making, planning, teamwork, and more, all of which are crucial for their personal and academic development.

We are looking forward to an enterprising week at Mohar Market Summer Camp.

Shraddha Chandwadkar keeps them engaged in games while they learn to be good team players, develop leadership qualities, and build self-esteem. Learning to be healthy through yoga and meditation is important in this life full of stress.

Mohua Maity, a master of strategy, will lead analytical chess sessions, honing your child's critical thinking skills. She also offers STEM programs, fostering a deep understanding of these subjects and boosting their confidence in decision-making.

Activities like arts and stem, chess, mindfulness, meditation, making Friday pizza, and watching movies will be included. For the rest of the four days, please send lunch and snacks with the kids.
