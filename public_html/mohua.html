Mohua Maity

Mohua is a Chess Instructor since 2014. She was associated with two different schools. Montessori School of Cumming and Cornerstone School (Georgia, USA).

She’s holding a total experience of 7 years, teaching kids aged 4-14 years. She’s also doing sessions for special kids.

  • In 1996, Mohua stood First in the Junior Chess Championship Tournament in Mumbai, India.
  • In 1997, she was ranked 7th in Maharashtra State Championship, India.
  • She organized Chess Tournament in 2019 in Atlanta, USA.
  • Currently, she's coaching students from different parts of the USA.
